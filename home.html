About

Findochty or "Finechty" is a small fishing village located on the north east coast of Moray, Scotland. One of the main unique characteristics of this small village is the white-painted church that sits atop the hill and overlooks the village from a height. Another characteristic of the village are the painted houses sporting many different colours. The village includes a scenic harbour which is overlooked by a war memorial on a hill opposite the white church. The popular 'Admirals' pub sits next to a caravan park which is always busy and full of tourists. Below is a map showing its location in Moray, Scotland.
