Essential Experiences

  • The Admirals Inn

    • Admirals is a cosy, friendly pub with great staff and superb homemade food. The bay windows in the conservatory overlook the sea and harbour.

  • Edindoune Rock

    • Perhaps go for a swim out to the rock of edindoune to view it in all its glory.

  • Bow Fiddle Rock

    • Instead maybe go to the cliffs of Portknockie, a neighbouring village, to see the famous "Bow Fiddle" rock.

  • Strathlene Golf Course

    • Go for a round of golf at the strathlene golf course, located just to the west of Finechty.

  • Cullen Beach

    • Take a walk on the beautiful beach of Cullen, a neighbouring village, and take in the sight and smell of the sea.
